Grown in BC

The perfect place in the world to grow.

Cooling ocean breezes and long hours of coastal sunlight make British Columbia one of the best regions in the world for greenhouse vegetable production — and our growers make the most of it, responsibly.

  • 96% of B.C.'s greenhouse vegetablesOur members grow the vast majority of the province's greenhouse produce.
  • More with lessNearly $500M in farm-gate value from an area smaller than Stanley Park.
  • Local since 1973Fresh, provincially grown produce for B.C. families, every season.
