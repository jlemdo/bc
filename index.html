Why greenhouses?

Our plants live in a health spa, 24 hours a day.

Step inside one of our glass greenhouses and you'll find a perfect tropical oasis, managed by science and technology. Our growers give plants exactly what they need — the right climate, light, water and nutrition — so our vegetables look and taste perfect. No waxes. Nothing unnatural. Just perfect.

  • A perfect climate, year-roundGrowers control the environment 24/7 so plants grow strong, healthy and big.
  • Grown hydroponicallyPlants stretch their roots in rockwool, sawdust and coconut fibre — biodegradable and efficient.
  • More harvest, less landWe grow more months of the year while making the most of B.C.'s coastal sunshine.

Meet the growers

The farmers for British Columbia's 21st century.

Since 1973, our members have grown from a small co-operative into one of B.C.'s agriculture success stories. Their skilled hands have turned science into art — growing world-class products in harmony with the environment.

The BC Greenhouse Growers' Association is committed to keeping our growers globally competitive, and our greenhouse vegetable sector vibrant and sustainable — while delivering safe, high-quality vegetables to families across the province.

Sustainable agriculture

Growing in harmony with the environment.

The cooling ocean breezes and long hours of sunlight make coastal British Columbia the perfect region in the world for greenhouse vegetable production — and our growers make the most of it responsibly.

  • Integrated pest managementBeneficial insects and natural methods keep crops healthy.
  • Water & energy stewardshipPrecise systems deliver exact amounts of water, CO₂ and nutrition.
  • Certified food safetyRigorous standards from seed to shelf, every single season.
